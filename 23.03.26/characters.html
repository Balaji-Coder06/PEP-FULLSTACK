Main Characters

The main characters of One Piece are the Straw Hat Pirates. Monkey D. Luffy is the captain, Roronoa Zoro is the swordsman, Nami is the navigator, Usopp is the sniper, and Sanji is the cook.

Straw Hat Crew

Other important members include Tony Tony Chopper, Nico Robin, Franky, Brook, and Jinbe. Each crew member has their own dream, personality, and role, making the team unique and lovable.
