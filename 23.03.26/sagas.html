Major Sagas

One Piece is divided into many major sagas such as East Blue Saga, Alabasta Saga, Sky Island Saga, Water 7 Saga, Thriller Bark Saga, Summit War Saga, Dressrosa Saga, Whole Cake Island Saga, and Wano Country Saga.

Story Progress

Each saga contains several arcs and shows the growth of Luffy and his crew. As the story moves forward, the enemies become stronger, the world becomes bigger, and the mystery of the One Piece becomes more exciting.
