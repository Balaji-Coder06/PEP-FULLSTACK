About One Piece

One Piece is a Japanese manga and anime series created by Eiichiro Oda. It follows Monkey D. Luffy, a young pirate who dreams of becoming the Pirate King by finding the legendary treasure called One Piece.

Why It Is Special

One Piece is loved for its adventure, powerful friendships, emotional backstories, comedy, world-building, and unforgettable characters. It has become one of the most popular and successful anime and manga series in the world.
